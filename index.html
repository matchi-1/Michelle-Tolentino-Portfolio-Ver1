about me

Year 2022, I joined a microcomputer innovation organized by The Department of Science and Technology, where the competitors harnessed ML to innovate tech that either addressed an untouched niche or provided accessible solutions for marginalized communities. That was the first time I got hooked to Computer science, specially into AI/ML, inspiring me to innovate, develop, and produce outputs that could be transformative.

For me, the appeal of this technology goes beyond its technical capabilities. It's the potential to give back to society that truly resonates with me. I believe that AI/ML is not just a personal passion, but a societal imperative.

Currently, I’m a 19-year old undergraduate at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila, actively involved in various volunteer organizations. I have practical experience in developing models for ML using python and have also contributed to several full-stack development projects. My skills extend to Back-End & Front-End Software Development, Database Management, and UI/UX design!

I've also proactively led teams and managed projects successfully, honing my project management skills over the years. Academically, I’ve consistently been a top student in my batch, with a cumulative GPA of 1.0924.
